CC3: Debating. Use a visualisation in economic/policy commentary

I chose the topic of UK exports as a debating/discussion point.

The total value of UK exports has been increasing over the years, but this increase has mainly been driven by strong growth in UK services exports.

The growth in services exports exceeding imports is mostly seen in nominal terms. However, the annualised percentage growth shows that service exports only exceeded goods exports in 2022
